About Wellness At Homes Care Services

Heartfelt Personal Assistance Across Texas

Wellness at Home Services was started by our founder’s genuine desire to help others. Seeing the difficulties faced by the elderly and disabled in finding quality care, and inspired by a crucial moment with a friend, our founder wanted to offer compassionate care right at home. We strive to provide the support needed to live with dignity and independence.

Why Choose Us?

With over 10 years of experience, our personal assistance service offers compassionate, tailored support for individuals with disabilities and those needing help with daily activities. We pride ourselves on delivering high- quality, personalized care that truly enhances each person’s quality of life.

  • We assist with bathing, dressing, and grooming.
  • We help with home and wheelchair transfers.
  • We prepare meals to meet dietary needs.
  • We manage light housekeeping and laundry.

Our Process

1. Initial Consultation

We start with a detailed consultation to understand your specific needs and preferences. This helps us tailor our services to ensure we meet your unique requirements and goals effectively.

2. Personalized Care Plan

Based on our consultation, we create a customized care plan designed to address your individual needs. This plan outlines the services we’ll provide and how we’ll ensure your comfort and well-being.

3. Ongoing Support And Adjustments

We regularly review and adjust your care plan to ensure it continues to meet your needs as they evolve. Our team provides ongoing support and communication to make sure you receive the best care possible.
